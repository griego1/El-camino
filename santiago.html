Highlights of the Cathedral

Main Façade

The Obradoiro façade, completed in 1740, is the iconic Baroque entrance seen by pilgrims arriving in Santiago.

Cathedral Interior

Inside the cathedral, visitors can see the altar, the botafumeiro, and the crypt of Saint James.

The Botafumeiro

This giant incense burner swings through the cathedral during special ceremonies, a tradition dating back centuries.

Tomb of Saint James

The tomb of Saint James (Santiago) is located in the crypt beneath the main altar of the Cathedral of Santiago de Compostela. It is one of the most important pilgrimage sites in Christianity, where pilgrims from all over the world visit to venerate the saint.
