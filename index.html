El camino de Santiago

The Camino de Santiago is a network of pilgrimage routes that lead to the Cathedral of Santiago de Compostela, Galicia, Spain , where the tomb of the Apostle James is located.Most of the routes pass through Spain, but there are also routes that go through France and Portugal. The most popular route is the French Way (Camino Francés). The other main routes include the Portuguese Way (Camino Portugués), the Northern Way (Camino del Norte), the Primitive Way (Camino Primitivo), the Silver Way (Vía de la Plata), the English Way (Camino Inglés), and the Finisterre Way (Camino Finisterre).
